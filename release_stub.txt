Two versions of the modpack are available:

- all_mods_download.zip: requires manual extract to `%USERPROFILE%\Documents\My Games\FarmingSimulator2019\Mods`.
- FermeCC_ModPack.exe: *recommended* a self-extrating archive requiring no other manual steps.

All mods are properties of their respective creators.

Entries marked with a star (*) indicate that changes were made to the original mod, either to enable multiplayer or for minor bugfixes. See [NOTES.md](https://github.com/FermeCC/ModPackBuilder/blob/master/NOTES.md) for more information.
